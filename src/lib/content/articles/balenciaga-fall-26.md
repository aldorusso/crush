# Front Row: Balenciaga Fall 26

The air in the 8th arrondissement was thick with anticipation. As the first notes of a mechanical drone filled the space, the world of luxury was once again reminded why Balenciaga remains the primary architect of the modern silhouette.

## The Architecture of Cloth

Demna's obsession with sculptural volume reached new heights this season. Using bonded silks and 3D-printed polymers, the collection felt less like garments and more like wearable monuments.
